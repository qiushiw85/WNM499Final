SmileCoffee

SmileCoffee is an app to help those who love coffee make their favorite drinks at home. Step by Step instructions walks a person through the recipes that scale between the American classics, dark or creamy drinks and flavored drinks that can go along with the change of seasons! Along with Ingredients list and recipes, you also have access to the History of the coffee drink you are interested in to heighten the experience to your coffee drinking. Cheers!!
